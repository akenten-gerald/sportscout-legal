Common questions

How do match alerts work?

Tap the bell on any match to get notified about lineups, kickoff, goals and lead changes, tight finishes, and the full-time result. Tap it again to turn alerts off. Notifications require the installed app with notifications allowed in your device settings.

How do I delete my account?

Open Profile → Account → Delete Account and confirm. This permanently removes your account and all associated data from our servers. You can keep using the app as a guest afterward.

Do I need an account?

No. Scores, the World Cup hub, scouting intel, and the AI assistant all work as a guest. Signing in (email, Apple, or Google) saves your favorites across devices and raises your daily AI message limit.

Why is some data delayed or missing?

Scores, schedules, and stats come from third-party providers and may briefly lag the broadcast or be temporarily unavailable. Pull down to refresh.
